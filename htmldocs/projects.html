Move It

Last summer two of my friends got married. Both of them are dancers and I wanted to make their wedding reception full of dance, music and happiness. I created a machine that played 30 second long versions of famous songs everytime the newly wed pressed a button. Whenever the music started everyone had to get up and dance. Platform: Raspberry Pi. Language: Python.
