About
Me

Creating is what I love the most. Thats why composing music and writing software is such a great fit for me. I really get a kick out of creating something and letting it take on a life of its own in the hands of the user or the listener.

Where I began

At the age of six I stumbled upon the most wonderful adventure a young boy could dream of. An adventure of the mind and of the heart! My grandmother taught me the first melodies I ever played on the piano. They where children songs that I could sing by heart, but to play them and to hear the melodies come from a different instrument instantly caught my interest. Ever since that day I have played the piano, and ever since that day I have composed my own melodies. My interest in music and sound grew and I soon started studying music. At first I took piano lessons, and as I grew older I continued to study music through high school. Immediately after finishing high school i applied for and got accepted into the Academy of Music in Malmö in Sweden. Well there I studied both composition and arrangement as well as singing and piano and also pedagogics in art. My heart truly lies with composing music and I absolutely love the synergy that comes from combining different art forms. This, and my interest for creating new sounds, has lead me to compose both acoustic and electronic music for rock and pop bands, dance choreographers, photographers, directors of film and other musicians. In 2008 I created the music company Valley Creek Music Productions. This is a firm concerning music production and artistic ventures such as composing and recording scores for movies, live events, session recordings for other artists and music consultation.
